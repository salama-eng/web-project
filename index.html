About Us

Welcome to the site of ANDALUSIAN CENTER located in University Place Washington state.at 8316 31 st, W university place WA 98466.

There is a great amount of refugees from the Middle East whose primary language is Arabic. They need assistance at schools to understand, especially when they first arrive in the US. The Andalusian Center provides interpretation services.

Establishment

Started in 2003 as a Project in Islamic Center of Tacoma. In 2007, it moved to Arabic Zoom Center. The Andalusian Center was established in 2016.

Subjects

Arabic Language, Culture Classes, Interpretation and Translation Services.

Schedules

Flexible Schedule based on student’s time.

Education divisions

Arabic Language

We offer modern standard Arabic according to the students’ level, age and time availability. There are also intensive sessions for those who are interested. Our teachers are qualified and experienced in the field of education.
